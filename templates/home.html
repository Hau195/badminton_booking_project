About

Welcome to Atlanta Badminton Club - Best Of Georgia. 3 years in a Row. At Atlanta Badminton Club, we believe that a healthy and active lifestyle is the key to happiness. Our mission is to provide the best Badminton experience for kids and adults. Whether you're a seasoned player or just starting your Badminton journey, our team of Coaches are here to support you every step of the way. From personalized training programs to group classes, we offer a variety of options to help you stay motivated and achieve your goals.
Dear Players of Atlanta, we thank you for supporting and getting Badminton into your life. We have been chosen as Honorable award winner in 2022 and as a Winner in 2023 & 2024. Thanks to you all for the support and vote.
